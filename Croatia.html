Croatia

Team Profile

Croatia had sealed their place in the finals by coming second in the group, to Iceland. They enter the tournament with a world ranking of 20 at the start of the tournament. The appearance of this team came as a surprise because they had not been expected to reach this far as of recent years(the semi-final). which is also, as of now, their best record having also reached 3rd place during the 1998 world cup

The squad is filled with many veteran professional players of proven quality, players such as: Luka Modric, Ivan Rakitic, Mario Mandzukic, and Ivan Perisic. All these players have many years of top level experience playing at the highest level for some of the world's best teams. This can help make the difference when going against the 3 other teams in the Semis as they field a younger, and less expereinced teams over-all
