Belgium

Team Profile

Belgium are rated as the "dark horse" of this years world cup finals. Belgium had comfortably secured their qualification by topping group H, which contains notable countries such as Greece and Bosnia and Herzegovina. Though they are touted as the dark horses of the tournament, the quality of players in the squad show that they have the tools to take the trophy home with them. The squad has been dubbed Belgium's "golden generatiom". Blessed with some of the modern games best offensive players such as Kevin De Bruyne, Eden Hazard, Thiabaut Courtois and Romelu Lukaku at the forefront of this golden generation

Belgium are ranked as 3rd in the FIFA world rankings coming in to the tournament. This tournament will be Belgium's 13th tournament finals they will be part of since their debut during the finals in the 1930s. Belgium are yet to win a world cup final, but the players who are all winners in their own respective clubs
