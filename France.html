FRANCE

Team Profile

France are ranked as the favorites to take it all home for the 2018 world cup. They had all but secured their spot during the qualification period by topping the group, which contained other notable nations such as:Sweden and the Netherlands. The squad is filled with promising youngsters such as Kyliand Mbappe, Ousmane Dembele and Thomas Lemar, as well as more established stars such as Paul Pogba, Antoinne Griezmann and Hugo Lloris.

Having previously won the title in 1998 the "Les Bleus" as they are called by the fans are seen as the favorites due to all the talent and squad depth they have on hand. This is to be the nations 15th appearance which makes them tied for the 6th most appearances in the tournament by any nation, they are tied with England and Spain. Even the manager, Didier Deschamps, has world cup winning pedigree as he was part of the French squad that had won the 1998 finals.
